Apparel Ordering, Built As A System

Four operational pillars that move apparel from idea to inbox — designed to keep programs organized and easy to manage as they scale.

01 — Design + Curation
01

Merchandise Design and Product Curation

Strong merch systems start with intentional design and thoughtful product selection. Nuclear builds apparel around audience, presentation, garment choice, and decoration method to create merchandise that feels cohesive, elevated, and built with purpose.

Graphics & layouts designed for the garment
Assortments built around audience & price
Cleaner brand representation through intentional planning
02 — Pop-Up + Campaigns
02

Pop-Up and Campaign Stores

Limited-run stores, event merchandise, special releases, seasonal campaigns, and short-term launches. Built to create momentum, increase engagement, and simplify apparel ordering during active campaigns.

Limited-time ordering windows & launches
Organized ordering during high-traffic campaigns
Drops, fundraisers, seasonal & event merch
03 — Always-On Stores
03

Always-On Stores

These are the foundational ordering systems that support ongoing apparel needs across an organization. This infrastructure supports both public-facing and private ordering environments, allowing different groups to access apparel in a structured, repeatable way.

Public & private ordering environments
Structured for repeat ordering at scale
Centralized access to approved products year-round
04 — Fulfillment + Inventory
04

Fulfillment and Inventory Support

As apparel programs grow, the challenge is no longer just decoration. It becomes organization, movement, readiness, and support. Nuclear helps manage the ongoing flow of apparel across always-on stores, internal ordering systems, event merchandise, stocked goods, and distribution channels in a way that keeps programs organized, accessible, and easier to manage.

Packing, shipping, fulfillment & distribution
Inventory systems for stocked & recurring merch
Structured operational support that scales
